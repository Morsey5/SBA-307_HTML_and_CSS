We Love Puppies!

But, lets talk about why having a furry companion is such a benificial venture. If you already have a furry companion, lets continue to spread the word to those who don't!

Where to start! We are aware that having any companion is equally benificial, but we choose in this instance to just highlight Dogs.

Most households in the United States have at least one pet. Studies have shown that the bond between people and their pets is linked to several health benefits.

Mental Health Benefits:

  • increasing your physical activity. Dog owners are likely to take their pets for a walk or run every day.
  • providing companionship.
  • reducing anxiety.
  • boosting self-confidence.
  • helping you meet new people.
  • adding structure to your day.

Things to look forward to:

  • They Provide Unconditional Love.
  • They Don't Judge You (for the most part).
  • They aren't always asking for something (maybe snacks).
  • They appreciate attention!!!!!!
  • They are protective.
